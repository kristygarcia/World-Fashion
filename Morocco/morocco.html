Fashion Tips


  • Dress conservatively
    • both out of respect and safety
    • especially when visiting religious sites

  • It can get hot
    • it's a good idea to bring light and airy clothes
    • maxi dresses and skirts as well as flowy pants wil be fashionable, conservative, and comfortable to wear in the heat

  • Bring scarves
    • They are cute and can make your outfits more interesting
    • They can be used as a heat protectant and will keep you warm when it gets cold at night

  • Comfortable Shoes are a MUST
    • Make sure to pack closed toe shoes to avoid dust from the streets and to easily walk on the ueaven roads
