Spain

Fashion Tips


  • It is best to stick with a neutral color palette
    • Beiges, browns, and crèmes
    • Maybe even add some bold and colorful accents
    • A common pattern duo is solids mixed with stripes

  • Spain locals maintain a clean look with their hair and clothes
    • So try to avoid baggy or oversized items
    • Fitted is the best way to go!

  • Depending on the time of year, bring a stylish coat that can go with a variety of outfits

  • Trousers are perfect due to their versatility

    • Locals remain loyal to their sport’s teams, so any jersey from a Spanish team works when blending in

  • For shoes comfortability is always best
    • Platform chunky sneakers are in!
    • Stylish sandals work well with most outfits as well

  • Trending in Spain still → Skinny Jeans!
