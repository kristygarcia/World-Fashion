Our Mission

With World Closet, we hope to provide fashion enthusiasts, xenophiles, and travelers alike with the resources to tap into their creativity and passions. We strive to be as authentic as possible in order to truly represent each of the unique countries that we are tasked with researching. Our website is a tool for expression and is not intended to force anyone to dress in a particular way. We hope to reach as many people as possible through this organization as teach the world about the cultures all around them.
