Fashion Tips


  • The style is dressy mixed with more casual and laid back elements
    • "Chic decontracte"
    • A dress with white sneakers
    • White jeans with blouses

  • Parisian color palette includes lots of neutrals with some pops of color
    • Florals and Gingham patterns
    • Mariniere tops
    • Plain t shirts

  • Don't go overboard with the accessories!

  • Well-fitted silhouettes
    • Blazers and trench coats
    • Maxi skirts and dresses

  • Avoid Flipflops!
